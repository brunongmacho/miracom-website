MiraLABEL

Case Coding,
That Eliminates Manual Labeling

Most facilities don't have a printing problem, they have an identification problem. Cases move too fast, labels fall off, and manual coding creates mistakes that follow products all the way to the customer.

The Problem

Bad codes slow down
the entire supply chain.

Unreadable Codes Cause Rejection

Smudged, faded, or poorly printed codes get rejected at sortation, at the dock, or worse — at the customer's receiving dock.

Speed Limits Hurt Throughput

Older coding systems can't keep up with modern line speeds. As production ramps up, codes get skipped or misaligned.

Wrong Codes Create Wrong Shipments

A single incorrect date code or batch number can flag an entire shipment for rejection — costing you the product and the customer.

Compliance Fails Result in Fines

Missing or incorrect lot codes, expiration dates, or country of origin markings trigger compliance violations and fines.

No Verification Means No Quality Control

If nobody checks whether a code printed correctly, bad codes keep moving downstream until someone finds them — usually the customer.

Rework Burns Labor and Time

When a case leaves your facility with a bad code, someone has to pull it, relabel it, and re-enter it into the system. That's double handling.
