The Problem

Sortation without control

Products Go To The Wrong Destination

A missed scan or routing error sends products to the wrong dock, pallet, lane, or customer.

Manual Sorting Cannot Keep Up

As volume increases, manual sorting becomes slower, less accurate, and harder to manage.

Bottlenecks Build Up Fast

One blocked lane or overloaded conveyor zone can create backups across the entire operation.

Product Flow Becomes Unpredictable

Supervisors know products are somewhere in the system, but they do not know exactly where, why they stopped, or what should happen next.

Errors Multiply Downstream

Wrong shipments, incomplete orders, mixed pallets, and delayed deliveries often begin with a sortation error upstream.

Equipment Without Intelligence Creates Waste

Conveyors can move products, but without real-time controls, sensors, and routing logic, they cannot optimize flow.
