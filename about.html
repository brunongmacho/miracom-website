About Miracom

Engineered Control Systems for Industrial Operations

Founded by Judah Holstein, Miracom Computer Corp. builds real-time control, tracking, and production intelligence systems for high-volume industrial environments. We don't do "innovative solutions." We build engineered systems that solve specific operational problems.

Our focus is simple: give operations teams real-time visibility, execution control, and production intelligence—so they can stop reacting to problems and start preventing them.

We've spent decades understanding the complexities of newspaper production and material handling operations. We know what happens when systems don't talk to each other. We know what lost control looks like. And we know how to fix it.
