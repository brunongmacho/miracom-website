About Miracom

Control Systems for Environments Where Delays Are Not an Option

Since 1995, Miracom Computer Corp. has been the leading supplier of post-press production controls and software for the newspaper industry — where missed deadlines mean missed customers.

We give you the tools to gather data on all aspects of your production line, so you can find opportunities to boost efficiency and cut costs. Our systems aren't just software running on servers — they're engineered control layers that interface directly with your production equipment.

Based in Eastchester, New York, we serve newspapers and packaging operations across North America with proven automation solutions and hands-on implementation support.

Our Approach

We Build Control Systems, Not Just Software

Miracom systems aren't just applications running on servers. They're engineered control layers that interface directly with production equipment—inserters, presses, conveyors, sortation systems.

We integrate with your existing hardware and software infrastructure. We don't ask you to rip and replace. We ask you to connect what you have—and gain control over what you couldn't see before.

  • Direct hardware integration with production equipment
  • Real-time data acquisition and monitoring
  • Seamless connectivity with planning systems
  • Proven reliability in high-volume environments
